How To Use The Wheel Of Anti Procrastination

To Assemble The Wheel Of Anti Procrastination you will need, your wheel, your base, and your support arm. Place the support arm in the base. Write the tasks you would like on your wheel with a dry earse marker and then pop the wheel on the support arm.
